Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 14, 2026

1. Introduction

WiseClick ("we", "us", or "our") operates the website insure.wiseclick.site. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you visit our website.

2. Calculator Data

We do not collect any data you enter into our calculators. All calculations are performed entirely in your web browser using JavaScript. Your information never leaves your device and is never transmitted to our servers.

3. Information We Collect

When you visit our website, we may automatically collect: browser type, operating system, pages visited, time spent, anonymized IP address, and device type. This is collected through standard web analytics tools.

4. Cookies

We may use cookies for: essential website functionality, analytics (e.g., Google Analytics), and advertising (e.g., Google AdSense). You can control cookies through your browser settings.

5. Affiliate Links

Our website contains affiliate links to third-party insurance providers. When you click an affiliate link, you will be directed to the provider's website. We do not collect or have access to any information you provide to insurance partners. If you submit information through a partner's quote form, that data goes directly to the insurance provider under their own privacy policy. We may receive a referral fee if you request a quote, at no additional cost to you.

6. Third-Party Services

Google AdSense: Displays advertisements. Google may use cookies to serve personalized ads. Opt out at Google Ads Settings.

Google Analytics: Analyzes website traffic using cookies. Data is aggregated and anonymous.

7. General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)

If you are a resident of the European Economic Area (EEA), you have the right to: access your personal data, request correction or deletion, restrict processing, data portability, and object to processing. We process data based on legitimate interests (website operation and analytics). Since we collect minimal data and calculator inputs never leave your browser, most GDPR rights are automatically satisfied. To exercise your rights, contact us at contact@wiseclick.site.

8. California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA)

California residents have the right to: know what information is collected, request deletion, opt out of data sales (we do not sell data), and exercise these rights without discrimination. Contact us at contact@wiseclick.site.

9. Children's Privacy

Our website is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. Changes will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.
