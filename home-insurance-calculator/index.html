How to Estimate Home Insurance Costs

Your premium depends on your home's value, age, construction type, location risk, and the coverage options you choose. Our calculator uses national average rates adjusted for these factors.

Ways to Save on Home Insurance

  • Raise your deductible from $500 to $1,000 for up to 15% savings
  • Install security systems and smoke detectors for discounts
  • Bundle with auto insurance for 10-20% off
  • Maintain a good credit score
  • Compare quotes from multiple insurers annually

Frequently Asked Questions

How much homeowners insurance do I need?

You need enough dwelling coverage to completely rebuild your home. Personal property coverage should be 50-70% of dwelling coverage. $300,000 in liability is typical.

What does homeowners insurance cover?

It covers damage to your dwelling, personal belongings, other structures, additional living expenses if displaced, and personal liability. Flood and earthquake usually require separate policies.

How can I lower my home insurance premium?

Raise your deductible, install security features, bundle with auto insurance, maintain good credit, and compare quotes from multiple insurers.
