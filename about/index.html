About WiseClick

Our Mission

WiseClick helps people understand insurance costs and make informed decisions. Insurance can be confusing, with complex terminology, varying coverage options, and prices that differ wildly based on personal factors. We built WiseClick to cut through that complexity with free, straightforward tools that give you real estimates you can actually use.

What We Offer

WiseClick provides 8 free insurance calculators covering the most common insurance needs:

  • Auto Insurance Calculator — Estimate your car insurance premiums based on your vehicle, driving history, and coverage preferences.
  • Life Insurance Calculator — Figure out how much life insurance coverage you need to protect your family.
  • Home Insurance Calculator — Get an estimate for homeowners insurance based on your property details.
  • Health Insurance Calculator — Compare health insurance costs and find the right plan for your situation.
  • Pet Insurance Calculator — Estimate pet insurance costs for dogs and cats based on breed, age, and coverage level.
  • Travel Insurance Calculator — Estimate travel insurance premiums based on trip cost, destination, and duration.
  • Insurance Needs Calculator — Assess your overall insurance coverage gaps and needs.
  • Deductible vs. Premium Calculator — Compare different deductible levels to find the right balance between monthly premiums and out-of-pocket costs.

Our Principles

  • Free Forever — No hidden fees, no premium tiers, no paywalls. Every calculator is completely free to use.
  • Privacy First — All calculations run in your browser. We never collect or store your personal insurance data.
  • Accuracy — Our estimates are based on national average data and industry-standard formulas to give you realistic numbers.
  • Accessibility — Our tools work on any device, anywhere, with no sign-up required.
